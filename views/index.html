Services

From accessible landing pages to enterprise-level development, I provide reliable and user-focused web solutions designed to elevate your digital presence.

Accessibility consultation

Need to make your website more accessible? I offer expert accessibility consultations to help your web application meet WCAG standards and become inclusive for users of all abilities. Using the Website Accessibility Conformance Evaluation Methodology (WCAG-EM), I provide a detailed accessibility review and deliver actionable recommendations on how to improve your site or app. Let’s make the web more inclusive—feel free to reach out for more details.

Landing page development

Need a clean, fast, and effective landing page to showcase your product or service? I create custom landing pages that are not only visually appealing but also optimized for accessibility, performance, and user engagement. Whether it’s for a campaign, event, or new launch, I’ll ensure your landing page delivers results across all devices and meets modern web standards. Get in touch to bring your idea to life!

Senior Angular Development & Consulting

Need an experienced developer to join your large-scale Angular project? I bring senior-level expertise to support your team with component architecture, accessibility best practices, and clean, maintainable code. Whether it’s a long-term collaboration or short-term support, I’m ready to step in and help move your project forward.
